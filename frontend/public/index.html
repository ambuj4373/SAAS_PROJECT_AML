Executive summary § 1

The British Red Cross Society is a 117-year-old auxiliary to the public authorities under the Geneva Conventions, registered with the Charity Commission since 1908. Filings show 24 trustees, total income of £301.4m for FY 2024, and an unqualified audit opinion CC API · §3.2. Sanctions screening across OFSI, OFAC and UN consolidated lists returned no matches for the entity or its 24 trustees OFSI · §6B.

One operational note: the charity reports activity in 14 jurisdictions, four of which carry elevated geographic risk under FATF guidance. These are reflected in the geography sub-score (12/100) and addressed in §4.

Governance & trustees § 5

Charity Commission records list 24 trustees as at the date of the most recent return CC API · 2024. The board is chaired by the President of the Society. All trustees were verified against OFSI, OFAC and UN sanctions lists; no matches.

The 2023 governance review, filed alongside the annual return, identifies independent trustee assessment, an active audit committee, and rotation cycles consistent with the Charity Governance Code.
