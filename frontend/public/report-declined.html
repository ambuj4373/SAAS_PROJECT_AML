Verdict§ 1 of 10

Do not proceed without enhanced due diligence and Treasury consultation.

Albion Maritime Holdings Ltd is a UK-incorporated company in the freight shipping sector that, on the basis of the public record alone, presents three independently serious findings — any one of which would warrant declining the relationship. The verdict on this report is do not proceed. The findings are: a sanctions match against the company’s majority Person of Significant Control on the OFSI consolidated list under the Russia regime; an active director who is currently subject to a Companies House disqualification order; and a first Gazette notice of compulsory strike-off filed by the Registrar on grounds of failure to file.

This is a sample report illustrating how a declined verdict is presented. Albion Maritime Holdings Ltd, its officers and PSCs are fictional and any resemblance to real entities or persons is coincidental.

Critical findings§ 2 of 10

Three findings drive the verdict on this report. They are not gradations of the same concern. Each is independently sufficient grounds to decline, and is sourced from the primary authority rather than aggregated reporting.

Critical finding B · Disqualified director
The sole UK-resident director is currently subject to a disqualification order.

The only UK-resident director on file, James Anthony Reid, appointed 04 November 2021, is recorded on the Companies House Disqualified Persons register with an active eight-year disqualification order made in 2019 Disq. register.

The disqualification arises from the conduct of a different company in the cargo logistics sector (now dissolved) and was made by the High Court under section 6 of the Company Directors Disqualification Act 1986. The order remains in force until 14 May 2027. Acting as a director while disqualified is a criminal offence under section 13 of the Act.

  • Reuters — 03 October 2025. Reporting on UK Treasury enforcement under the Russia regime cites the Volkov designation as an example of designations subsequently linked to UK-incorporated entities. Albion is mentioned by name Reuters · 03 Oct 2025.
  • BBC News — 08 October 2025. Regional reporting referencing port authority hold notices on a vessel said to be chartered by Albion subsidiaries in the previous twelve months BBC · 08 Oct 2025.

Methodology & sources§ 10 of 10

What this report is — and isn’t.

This is research-grade intelligence, not regulated AML advice. The findings above are material on their face and warrant either declining the relationship outright or, where the relationship cannot be declined, enhanced due-diligence procedures and consultation with the UK Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation regarding the Volkov designation.

Why we publish a sample of a declined report.

Probitas is only useful if it is willing to deliver bad news clearly. This sample exists so that prospective customers can see what a declined verdict looks like — not just a cleared one. The visual treatment, the language, and the structure of findings on a high-risk report are deliberately distinct from a cleared report so the verdict is unmistakable at a glance.
